### 129/1.
$v = 100 \frac{km}{s}$
$m_e = 9,1*10^{-31} kg$
$e_0 = 1,6*10^{-19} As$

$F_m=F_r$ - Magnetna sila ima vlogo radialne (centripetalne)
$evB = ma_r$
$evB = m\frac{v^2}{r}$
$eB = \frac{mv}{r}$
$B = \frac{mv}{er} = \frac{9,1*10^{-31}kg*100*10^3\frac{m}{s}}{1,6*10^{-19}As*2*10^{-2}m} = 2,8 * 10^{-5}T$
